MY WORK

During the recent pandemic, many workers were forced to leave their offices and work from home. While some may have struggled with this new environment, others saw the value in the flexibility for themselves and their families. As some workers returned to the office, many more chose another path. As we enter the post-pandemic era these workers have become a whole new category of the working force, coined the Digital Nomad. They spend their full time schedule working on the go! Our team is seeking to tackle the problem of reliable working space for traveling workers.

There are so many skills that you don't learn in school. How do you do your taxes? How do I organize my life? Wait, you have to wash your diswasher?! Why can't you have one place where you can learn it all? Our users want the confidence and independence to navigate the adult world in a simple and organized way. Adult-Tings is here to help.

My team and I chose the USDA Website to redesign because of its vast variety of information that needed organizational improvements for a better user experience.  We conducted usability testing on the current design, and with our findings, prototyped a new layout. Through additional usability testing, we iterated on our original redesign to make it as clear and easy to navigate as possible.
